Hello there!

I'm a recent graduate of Carnegie-Mellon and Madeira University's dual-degree professional Masters of HCI program.

I'm currently living in Lexington, Kentucky with my awesome husband!

I am passionate about designing and researching educational games using time-tested instructional design priciples and HCI research methods.

Previously, I created websites in Lexington, KY where I also studied human factors, cognition, and learning at the University of Kentucky.

Occasionally, I'll answer questions related to programming and user experience.
