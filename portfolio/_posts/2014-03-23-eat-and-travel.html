Project Summary

This was my capstone project for the Double Masters of Human-Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon University and the University of Madeira.

We completed this project over two semesters with another team of students from the Masters of Entertainment Technology program and our sponsor, a Lisbon-based startup called Eat & Travel.

Project Roles

Project Manager User Researcher Progammer Designer

My Roles

At different times during this project we each took responsibility for one of the major roles. It was as a good opportunity to gain more real-world experience.

Project Management

During the first semester, my primary role was project manager. I was the primary liason between our client and faculty, I kept the project on track, was the primary presenter, and I made sure all are deliverables were completed.

User Research

During both semesters, I contributed in recruiting participants, study design, conducting studies, collecting data, and analyzing results.

Programming

The first semester's focus was on design and the second semester was implementation. I contributed to several low-fi digital prototypes and completing the final prototype.

Design

I contributed to design in a supporting role. I designed elements of presentations, print materials, infographics for our client newsletter, photography, and I was the lead on our final concept video.
