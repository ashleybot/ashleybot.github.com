Project Summary

This was my capstone project for the Double Masters of Human-Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon University and the University of Madeira.

We completed this project over two semesters with another team of students from the Masters of Entertainment Technology program and our sponsor, a Lisbon-based startup called Eat & Travel.
