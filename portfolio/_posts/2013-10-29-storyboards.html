Project Summary

This project was completed as an assignment for Interaction Design Fundamentals at Carnegie Mellon University.

The project was designed so that we brainstormed quickly as a group and then separated for an individual work.

Techniques Used

Bodystorming Product Development Synthesis Quick Storyboarding

Bodystorming

My group used bodystorming to discover scenarios that common to seniors.

Bodystorming is a technique very similar to brainstorming. In bodystorming, improv elements ensure that ideas are situated in the proper context.

Within an hour, we had 25 scenarios unique to seniors.

Synthesis

I later synthesized those scenarios to organize common themes among them.

I discovered four themes of importance:

  • Relationships
  • Nostalgia
  • The Mind
  • The Body

Product Development

I kept these ideas in mind as I brainstormed different products which would support as many scenarios as possible.

My strongest idea was the Communication Mug. It is a video phone inside of a color-coded mug.

Quickly Creating Storyboards

Storyboards are a useful tool to communicate ideas easily to a team. Designers and developers can gain a united vision quickly.

They can be sketched out quickly on paper or on a whiteboard.

With some minimal adjustments in Photoshop, the sketch can be ready for use.

Depending on the product and the audience, more meaning can be conveyed through color and other aesthetics.

Final Storyboard

The final storyboard communicates that the mug aids the aging mind, it provides opportunities to create nostalgic events, and puts relationships first.

  • The Communication Mug is a product for seniors living retirement homes.
  • It provides a way to seniors to connect with other residents.
  • And stay in touch with family far away.
