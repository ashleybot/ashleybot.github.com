Project Summary

This project was created during an Interaction Design Fundamentals course at Carnegie Mellon University. We flexed our newly acquired design skills in this final project.

My Contributions

Brainstorming Competitive Analysis Situation Awareness and Navigation Mood Board Programming

Our Goals

Myself and two of my classmates worked together to reveal a gap in the market for our magazine, we achieved a cohesive design, and gained more experience with design tools like Illustrator.

Competitive Analysis

We began our project with a thorough competetive analysis of two markets: bird magazines and nature magazines.

We found that the market for bird magazines, from bird watching enthusiasts to pet owners, is saturated. So, we focused on nature magazines.

We saw a long array of children's nature magazines as well as adults. However, there was an anomaly between the two. A gap existed in children's hunting magazines.
