The Grand Abyss

Yesterday, I lost my car key somewhere between 1.5 hours and 5 miles of forest.

After the initial oh-shit gut reaction, my thoughts went to which search algorithm I could employ to most efficiently retrace my steps and find my key.

Hurl yourself into the grand abyss.
Unknown

I knew I had the key about 1 mile into the beginning of the hike and I had three traversal options:


  1. Start from the beginning

  2. From the end

  3. Or use the many artery trails to subdivide the main trail

At this point, the avid reader is noticing an important detail which changes the entire story.

There are other hikers on the trail.

I didn’t realize this until my key was miraculously returned to me. The kind couple who gave me the key were given the key by another couple (who were given the key by another couple – jk).

I needed a packet routing algorithm all along! Silly me.

It’s not as much abyss as it is grand.
